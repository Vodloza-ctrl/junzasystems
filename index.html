FAQ

Questions we get every day

Do I need any technical skills to use a Junza bot? +

None at all. We handle everything — strategy, build, testing, launch, and updates. You just share your business context and we do the rest. If you can use WhatsApp, you can run a Junza bot.

How long does it take to go live? +

Typically 5–7 business days from the strategy call to live deployment. Complex bots with payment integrations or multi-branch logic may take up to 10 days. We'll give you a clear timeline upfront.

Will the bot work on my existing WhatsApp number? +

Yes. We build on WhatsApp Business so your existing number and customer relationships stay intact. No need to move customers to a new number or platform.

Can I try the bot before I buy? +

Absolutely. Our live demo bots are linked above — try them right now, for free. They're real bots we built for actual businesses, not mockups. What you see is what you get.

What if I want a bot for multiple branches or locations? +

We handle multi-location setups and white-label models for agencies managing multiple clients. Contact us on WhatsApp to discuss your specific needs and we'll build a custom plan.

Do you serve businesses outside Zimbabwe? +

Yes. Junza is built for African businesses first, but we serve clients regionally and internationally. If your customers use WhatsApp, we can build for you.
